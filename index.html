EVOLVE MMA GYM

About

EVOLVE is a mixed martial arts (MMA) gym that is well known for producing world champions. MMA is a combat sport that, as its name suggests, involves multiple disciplines of martial arts including Boxing, Muay Thai, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Judo to name a few. EVOLVE MMA offers many convinient classes and state-of-the-art modern facilities. They also boast having the biggest team of experienced instructors in Asia.

They have many branches around Singapore for your convenience:

  • Far East Square
  • Orchard Central
  • KINEX
  • PoMo Mall

EVOLVE MMA (Far East Square)

This branch is the flagship location and the biggest MMA academy in Asia. It offers a 20 000 square foot training facility with high quality equipment like a Official Octagon MMA Cage from U.S.A, Muay Thai Heavy Bags from Thailand and 300 full size lockers.
