Programmes @ Far East Square



Muay Thai

Muay Thai is a martial art that originated from Thailand. It has a long history and is known for being very powerful. It consists of kicks, punches, knees, and elbows. EVOLVE has world champion instructors from Thailand and offers different classes for beginers, intermediate, advanced and competition level. Trial classes are available.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, better known as BJJ, is a grappling-based martial art that uses controlling positions, joint locks and chokes. BJJ is one of the most potent, real-life fighting systems invented taking into account that most real-life fights end up being on the ground. EVOLVE offers world champion instructors and different classes for beginers, intermediate, advanced and competition level. Trial classes are available.

Boxing

Boxing is one of the oldest and most efficient combat sports. It involves punches, footwork and head movement. The key is to hit and not get hit. EVOLVE offers world champion instructors and different classes for beginers, intermediate, advanced and competition level. Trial classes are available.
